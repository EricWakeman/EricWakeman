Aspiring Actor >> Animal Welfare Official >> Software Developer! How does a Theatre major wind up writing code? Long story short: I initially studied Theatre at The College of Idaho with dreams of doing voice-work for video games and animation. Life events cut my studies short, but I found a second home at the Idaho Humane Society where I stayed for five years. After my most recent 3 years serving as an Animal Welfare Officer, I realized I needed a change. Though I loved the work, helping the animals of Ada County and their owners, I needed to shift gears to a career where I could find work-life balance and look after my health. Many of my close friends from College are in the field of developing software, and with my strength in pattern recognition and logical problem solving, I believed coding might be a good fit for me. Now having completed CodeWorks' Immersive Full-Stack Web Development Course, I can proudly call myself a Software Developer, and say that learning to code was one of the best decisions of my life!
